Paracetamol : a pain reliever and antipyretic, it works by inhibiting cyclooxygenase in the body, paracetamol or (acetaminophen) is a pain reliever, its mode of action is not known precisely,
The most important uses of paracetamol and caffeine
For pain in general, a combination of paracetamol (acetaminophen) and caffeine is used to treat: The pain is like a headache. muscle pain; Cramps and menstrual pain. arthritis. Rheumatic pain. Pain and heat associated with colds and flu. Back pain and toothache. Postoperative pain
What are the contraindications of using Paracetamol Extra?
It is forbidden to use the drug without consulting a specialist doctor in the following cases: Hypersensitivity to any of the components of the drug. Patients with severe liver failure, and patients with severe liver disease or liver failure. Children under 12 years old.
What are Paracetamol doses and methods of use?
The recommended dose for adults is 1-2 tablets per day.
